The New York Times
Print · Digital · OOH · Video · Events
As Co-Manager of the Integrated Production Studio, my role was to work directly with the NY Times Creative Marketing team and Book Development team to produce content for Print, Digital, OOH and Video advertisements. In the Studio we produced content for New York times Cooking, Games, Best Seller Novels, Podcasts and Times Center events as well as rush Ground Breaking News instances. This included Newspaper ads, Digital Display, OOH and Event Signage and Video. Most notably, the New York Times Grand Central Station takeover, which included subway train wraps (interior and exterior), specialty banners and turnstile wraps.

Rolex
Print · Lifestyle · Special Events
Working directly with the Rolex Marketing Department, I managed the production of the Rolex co-op collaborative ads initiative. This consisted of creating and managing a library of all campaign models and size variations for various publications. From the start of the campaign all images were color corrected and retouched before being placed in mechanicals. Jeweler logos were added and released to hundreds of publications, both National and Local. Occasional lifestyle and special events ads were produced at Rolex's request.
